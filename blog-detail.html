Designing visuals that win approvals

By Alex Rivera · Jan 10, 2026 · 8 min read

Introduction

Stakeholder alignment is the most critical phase for architectural projects. Visuals that tell a story reduce ambiguity and unlock approvals faster.

Strategy: Start with the narrative

Use visuals to highlight purpose, experience, and context. Focus on the people, not just the building.

Workflow: Short feedback loops

Introduce structured reviews with clear milestones. Annotated feedback tools help clients stay aligned.

Delivery: Present the full story

Deliver imagery, animations, and VR previews together for a cohesive campaign.
